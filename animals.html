Animals

An animal is a living organism that belongs to the biological kingdom Animalia. Animals are characterized by their ability to move voluntarily, consume organic matter for energy, and respond to stimuli.

  • Wild Animals
  • Domestic Animals
  • Semi Domestic Animals

Wild Animals

Wild animals live in natural habitats like forests, mountains, oceans, and deserts. They survive on their own without human care.

  1. Carnivores Ex: Lion,Tiger,Hyena
  2. Herbivores Ex: Deer,Zebra,Elephant
  3. Omnivores Ex: Bear,Monkeys

Domestic Animals

These animals are raised by humans for companionship, farming, or labor. They have been selectively bred over generations for desirable traits.

  1. Pets Ex: Cat,Dog,rabbits
  2. Farm Animals Ex: Cows,Chicken,Sheep
  3. Work Animals Ex: Horse,Donkey,Oxen

Semi Domestic Animals

These animals are often kept by humans but still retain some wild instincts.

  • Ex: Parrots,Pigeons,Gold Fish
